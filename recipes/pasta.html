Let's see how to cook Red sauce pasta(My style)

Preparation

  • In a sauce pan boil water and add 100 gms of pasta(add a pinch of salt ) (Boil Until soft (Around 10mins))
  • Drain the water from it and keep it aside for now
  • Fine chop 2-garlic cloves, 1 inch ginger , half a serano chilli and one medium sized onion.
  • Cut cube sized capsicum(Red and Green)
  • Take 25gms of All purpose flour and mix it will with one cup water.
  • Fix the Flame to medium

Cooking

  • Heat up 2 table spoon oil(olive preferably). Put in the finely chopped garlic, ginger and chilli until garlic turns light brown.
    Add Onion stir fry till golden brown . Then add the cut capsicum, stir fry till it's soft(about 3-4 minutes)
  • Add salt, Pepper and other spices acc to taste
  • Optional: Add 4-5 tsps of tomato paste
  • Add 2 tsps of soya sauce and 4 tsps of tomato ketchup
  • Mix it well . Add the flour water mix to it and a lil more water and about 30 ml of milk
  • Add the pasta in the sauce. Keep cooking until sauce is thick. At last reduce the flame and cover it(4-5 minutes).
  • Ready to serve
